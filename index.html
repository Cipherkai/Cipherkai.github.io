Recipe #1

  • Time: 20:00
  • Serves:20
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Either kill or buy some chicken

Step 2: Cut the chicken up into medium sized pieces.

Step 3: Put flour on the chicken.

Step 4: Put the chicken into the frying pan.

Step 5: DEEP FRY THE CHICKEN; for approximatly 10 minutes.

Step 6: Use a spactula to grab the chicken out of the pan.

Step 7: Serve the chicken with rice.

Step 8: Enjoy the meal.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 20:00
  • Serves:4
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Get some eggs, cheese, onions, basicaly anything that goes well with eggs.

Step 2: Fry onions and other veggies.

Step 3: Mix eggs and season them.

Step 4: Put the eggs with the frying veggies.

Step 5: Wait about 3-4 minutes and put a top on the pan.

Step 6: Wait a few minutes, and flip the egg.

Step 7: Grab egg out of the pan.

Step 8: Enjoy the meal.
